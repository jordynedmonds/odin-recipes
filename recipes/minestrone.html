Minestrone

Description

This classic italian dish is packed with nutritious veggies and is absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 Quart of chicken stock
  • 1 Can of Cannelini Beans
  • 4-5 Whole tomatoes
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 3 Carrots
  • 3 Celery Stalks
  • 1 Onion diced

Steps

  1. Heat a 1/4 cup of olive oil in pot.
  2. Dice carrot, onion, and celery and saute for 10 minutes.
  3. Add the rest of the vegetables and chicken stock.
  4. Cover and bring to a boil.
  5. Once it starts boiling, remove the lid and reduce to a simmer and let it cook for 30 minutes
