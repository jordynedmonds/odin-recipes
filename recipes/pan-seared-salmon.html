Pan Seared Salmon

Description

This salmon is flaky and tender while having crispy skin!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Avocado oil
  • Lemon
  • Flaky sea salt

Steps

  1. Bring salmon to room temp
  2. Pat salmon dry on both sides
  3. Squeegee any excess moisture on salmon skin side with knife
  4. Heat avocado oil in pan over medium high heat
  5. Place salmon in pan skin side down and apply pressure to keep salmon from buckling
  6. Baste flesh side of salmon with hot oil
  7. Cook salmon to desired temp
  8. Take salmon off heat and sprinkle skin side with flaky sea salt and lemon juice
